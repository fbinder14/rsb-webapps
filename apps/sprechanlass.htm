Scene 1 — At the beginning of the day

How to use in class

  1. Students work in pairs or groups of 2–3.
  2. Teacher (you) selects a tab. Turn Focus on to hide the other scenes.
  3. Students create their own sentences and questions using the key words. 3–6 lines per person.
  4. Tap any key word to show a quick explanation (works on iPad & phones).
  5. Groups rehearse and act out their scenes for the class.
